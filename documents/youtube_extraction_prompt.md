### Key Principles (Summary)

**Author Portraits:**
- Focus on PERSON (life, struggles, character), NOT their work
- 150-300 word biographical paragraph
- Chronological, emotional depth
- Save to: `/output/author_portraits.md`

**Metadaten (5 tags at END):**
```
#author: [Name]
#published: [Month Year]
#source: [URL]
#background: [Biographical paragraph about PERSON'S LIFE]
#decade: #[peak influence decade]
```

**Document Structure:**
- 10 action-oriented numbered points
- German without Anglicisms (or English)
- 70-110 lines total
- Metadata at END, not beginning

**Critical:** #background describes the LIFE/JOURNEY, not the work
