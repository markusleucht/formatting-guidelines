# Lee Robinson

## Bio

Lee Robinson is a developer and writer currently working at Cursor, where he teaches about AI. Previously, he was employed at Vercel. With 15 years of coding experience and a second half of his career dedicated to teaching, his mission centers on making technology accessible and engaging.

Beyond his professional work, Lee is a husband, father, and music enthusiast. He actively creates content across multiple platforms and engages in advisory roles and angel investing.
