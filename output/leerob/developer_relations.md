# On Developer Relations

Developer Relations represents the convergence of programming expertise, education, and community engagement. It's an opportunity to channel your technological passion into supporting a broader community and facilitating their growth.

## What do they do?

* **Community:** DevRel professionals serve as community organizers. They facilitate gatherings and maintain active presence in community spaces, enabling developers to ask questions, exchange insights, and advance collectively. The community transforms into your product's strongest advocates.

* **Education:** These professionals teach the developer population. Whether through documentation, video content, or sample projects, they build awareness and enhance developer knowledge. This work demands both helpfulness and transparency.

* **Product:** Developer Relations practitioners significantly influence product direction. They translate developer challenges into actionable roadmap items. Beyond documentation responsibilities, this involves genuine interest in improving the programmer experience. Success requires deep empathy with developer needs.

## What makes a great DevRel?

* You love building.
* You love writing.
* You are an exceptional engineer and you love learning new technology.
* You have a passion for technology and a desire to share it with others.
* You enjoy talking about technology, while having empathy for others views.
* You enjoy helping others. You give more than you take.
